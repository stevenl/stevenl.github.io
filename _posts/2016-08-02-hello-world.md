So I decided to resurrect my blog. While I was at it I discovered this cool blogging platform called [Jekyll](https://jekyllrb.com/).

It takes a different approach to the traditional CMS. Your posts are written as `markdown` files. It generates static web pages instead of pulling data out from a database, which means it will feel much more lightweight. As a bonus I can serve it for free from my GitHub account... OK, I admit it! The appeal of Jekyll is that it's another tech toy.

More to come soon.
